Lasagna

Description

This is a tasty dish from Italian origins

It is a very simple dish consisting of different layers of pasta, meat sauce, and cheese, repeated

Ingredients

  • Lasagna Pasta Sheets
  • Tomato sauce
  • Ground Beef
  • Parmesan Cheese

Steps

  1. Cook Pasta
  2. Make a meat sauce with the tomato sauce and ground beef
  3. Place a layer of cooked pasta
  4. Place a layer of meat sauce
  5. Place a layer of cheese
  6. Repeat Steps 3-5 until satisfied
